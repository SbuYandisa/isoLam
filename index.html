What We Do
Tutoring

Recognizing the challenges that students may encounter in their academic journey, we offer comprehensive support across all subjects. Our tutoring programs are conducted in collaboration with esteemed organizations to ensure a holistic and effective educational assistance.

Mentorship

Our mentorship program is designed to guide learners in making well-informed decisions regarding academic pursuits, career paths, and other aspects of life that may impact their academic and student experiences.

Career Guidance

Career guidance is a pivotal component in shaping an individual's professional journey. It involves a systematic process of assessing one's skills, interests, and values to make informed decisions about career choices.

University Applications

Comprehensive support for university applications is available, encompassing essential information and guidance throughout the application process. We provide assistance to learners, ensuring they are well-equipped to navigate the complexities of university applications with confidence and clarity.

About Isolam

Our Mission

Our mission at Isolam is to empower high school students as they transition to higher learning institutions. We are committed to providing comprehensive support, guiding students through the application processes for admission and bursaries. Our objective is to equip them with essential tools necessary for a successful journey beyond high school. Under the mentorship of graduates from various universities across South Africa, we strive to foster academic excellence and personal growth, ensuring that every student is well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in their educational pursuits.

Our Vision

Through collaboration with diverse sponsors, our aspiration is to extend our reach to every student in South Africa, facilitating the development of their careers. This initiative commences with aiding students in establishing and attaining their goals. We achieve this through the implementation of rigorous tutoring, comprehensive mentorship programs, and strategic fundraising efforts to support their educational pursuits.
