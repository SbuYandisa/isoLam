About Us

Isolam, Non-Profit Organization

Founded by a group of students who encountered diverse challenges throughout their academic journeys, this initiative emerged from their collective experiences. Through mutual support from high school to university, their objective is to positively influence the lives of fellow students, facilitating a more assured and practical navigation of both academic and career paths.

Founded
Backround Story

Navigating the selection of a suitable career path is a complex endeavor, as the choices made invariably influence one's life trajectory. Many students find themselves compelled to either take a agap year or persue courses at higher institutions that do not align with their true passions. This often arises from a lack of proper guidance, leading to instances of student dropout, academic exclusion, and the attaainment of qualifications that do not resonate with personal interests. in repsonse to these challenges, Isolam was established by a collective of individuals who underwent similar experiences during their academic journeys. Drawing from their mutual support system, cultivated through high school and university. The organisation is committed to effecting positve change in the lives of other students.

Mission

Our mission is to help high school students prepare for life at higher learning institutions. This includes assisting them in applying for admission and bursaries; and to equip them with all the necessary tools they need to navigate life after high school, all under the guidance of graduates from various universities across South Africa.

Vision

Through the help of various sponsors and assistance from graduates, we hope to reach every student in South Africa and help develop their careers, starting with assisting them to set goals and help them achieve these goals through tutoring, mentorship programs, career guidance, and raising funds for their education.
